About Me

I completed my PhD at University of Colorado at Boulder in the Computer Science department. I work in computer security, more specifically in anti-censorship. I was advised by Eric Wustrow.

Previously I received a Master of Science degree in Mathematics from the University of Pittsburgh. I studied broadly in the areas of Algebra, Combinatorics and Number theory. I was advised by Jeff Wheeler, my thesis was on the Collatz Conjecture (3n+1 problem).
